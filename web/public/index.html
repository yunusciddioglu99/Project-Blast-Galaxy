Blast Galaxy is the ultimate destination for groups and companies looking for a day filled with classic arcade fun. We’ve redefined the traditional experience in a modern arcade club, while safeguarding the soul and spirit of the classic arcade – combining a great atmosphere and a quality and creative menu with a unique selection of more than 100 amazing arcade games.

We're open the following hours:
Wednesday: 12.00-18.00
Thursday: 16.00-23.00
Friday: 16.00-00.30
Saturday: 12.00-00.30

We’re closed to the general public several days a week, allowing you to have the place all to yourselves. The perfect setting for a group to enjoy our food & drinks, and all our arcade games, either at your own pace of guided by our team with a fun tournament. Blast Galaxy also serves as a one-of-a-kind backdrop for any event – such as press and launch events, video shoots, or e-sports competitions – and offers a private area for presentations and meetings.

We are able to completely customize the experience and can create tailor made packages, for any needs and budget. Please get in touch with us to find out more!

One entrance fee, all our arcade and console games are free!
